About Me:

I have always had a spark for problem-solving, always finding maths and physics to be the most engaging subjects in school. I've also had a curiosity for computer technology from a young age, and after watching my dad replace a piece of hardware in the family computer as a child, It wasn't long before I was taking a screwdriver to every PC tower I could find. This naturally progressed to building myself a custom PC tower and assisting friends in building their own by the age of 12. Over the years my interest in computer technology led to becoming a hobbyist software developer. After several years of working in many different positions, I decided To get myself on the road to a new career I am passionate about, so I chose to start a self-funded development course with Just IT. After completing a couple of practice projects, I am excited and ready to move into my first junior developer role and continue to build on my skills.

Education and introduction to work

Whilst completing my education at South Bromsgrove Sixth Form, I worked in the hospitality sector, and after finishing my A-levels I managed to land a job working in the distribution centre of one of the UK's fastest-growing companies, Gymshark, which led me to the next step in my career.

Gymshark - Brand Executive

After less than a year working in the distribution centre, I was promoted and began working as a logistics link for the brand team in the head office. I stayed in this role for more than 2 years, allowing me to build upon my inter-personal and problem-solving skills. I loved the fast-paced business environment and met a lot of inspirational people, which inspired me to move into a career I am truly passionate about.

Studying with JustIt

After a lot of planning and saving, I left my role at Gymshark to take some time out travelling in South America, then follow my dreams by starting a full-time, self-funded software development course with Just IT. As I continue to build my skill set, my excitement to move into the sector only grows.
